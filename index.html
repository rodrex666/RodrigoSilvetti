About me

I'm Rodri a XR Developer and Game Designer who loves turning complex ideas into fun experiences. My path started in systems engineering, took a detour through Germany for a personal challenge ( to learn the language!), and landed me in the awesome world of Digital Reality with a Master's degree.

My tools of choice are Unity and Unreal Engine 5. I'm currently channeling my love for music into a hand-tracking VR instrument, and my next quest is to rebuild it in UE5 to include gamified music lessons. I'm a firm believer that the best way to learn is by playing, and I bring that same fun-first, relaxed approach to my teamwork. I do my best work when the vibe is chill and collaborative.
